About Derivatives Navigator

Derivatives Navigator was founded to fix structural and behavioural problems in retail F&O trading identified by SEBI. We combine research, systems design, and risk-first education to reduce avoidable losses.

Our mission

To reduce avoidable retail losses by teaching evidence-based, compliance-first derivatives education.

Founder

Tejas Nikhar — Founder & Chief Research Strategist. 18+ years in markets, quant & retail education. He leads research, curriculum design and mentorship.

Our approach

  • Evidence-first curriculum, mapped to regulator findings.
  • System design & validation before execution.
  • Risk-first frameworks; no tips or live trading recommendations.
